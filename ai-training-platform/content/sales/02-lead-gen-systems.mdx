## The Death of "Spray and Pray" (Why Generic Doesn't Work)

Let's talk about the old way of lead generation and why it doesn't work anymore.

### The Old Way (Why It Fails)

**"Spray and Pray" approach:**
- Send 1000 generic emails
- Hope some people reply
- Most get deleted or marked as spam
- Low response rates (1-2% if you're lucky)
- Damages your reputation

**The problem:**
- People are overwhelmed with generic emails
- Spam filters are smarter
- Recipients can spot generic content instantly
- It doesn't work anymore

### The New Way (Relevance at Scale)

**What you need:**
- **Relevance**: Emails that matter to the recipient
- **Scale**: Ability to send many personalised emails
- **AI**: Makes personalisation at scale possible

**The solution:**
AI allows you to personalise every single message based on data. You get relevance AND scale.

*'Old Way: 1000 generic emails, 1% response. New Way: 1000 personalised emails, 10% response. AI: I can help you personalise at scale!'*

## AI Personalisation at Scale (The Secret Sauce)

This is where AI really shines. Don't just insert `{{FirstName}}`. Use AI to create truly personalised messages.

### The Old Way (Why It's Obvious)

**Generic personalisation:**
```
Hi {{FirstName}},

I hope this email finds you well. I wanted to reach out about our amazing product...

Best,
{{YourName}}
```

**Why it fails:**
- Obvious it's automated
- Doesn't show you know anything about them
- Gets deleted immediately
- Feels spammy

### The New Way (True Personalisation)

**Step 1:** Get lead data (LinkedIn profile, company info, recent activity)

**Step 2:** Ask AI to personalise:
> "Read this LinkedIn bio: [Insert Bio]. Write a 1-sentence icebreaker that compliments them on a specific achievement. Keep it casual. Don't sound like a stalker."

**Step 3:** Use AI-generated opener in your email

**Example output:**
> "Saw you just expanded to Brisbane â€“ congrats on the growth!"

**Much better than:** "I hope this email finds you well."

### The Complete Personalised Email

**Ask AI:**
> "Write a cold email to [Name] at [Company]. They're a [Title] at a [Company Type]. I noticed [specific detail from their LinkedIn/company]. My product helps [solve their likely problem]. Use the PSA framework: Problem, Solution, Action. Keep it under 150 words. Sound like a helpful colleague, not a salesperson."

**What you get:**
- Truly personalised email
- Relevant to their situation
- Natural, not robotic
- Much more likely to get a reply

*'Generic Email: *gets deleted* AI-Personalised Email: *gets opened* Recipient: This person actually knows about me. I'll reply.'*

## The PSA Framework (Problem, Solution, Action)

This is a simple but powerful framework for cold emails.

### The Framework

**P - Problem:**
Identify a problem they likely have (based on their role, company, industry)

**S - Solution:**
Present your product as the solution (briefly, benefit-focused)

**A - Action:**
Clear, low-pressure call to action

### Example Using PSA

**Problem:**
> "I see you're hiring for sales, which usually means chaos in the CRM."

**Solution:**
> "We automate the data entry so reps can just sell."

**Action:**
> "Worth a chat?"

**See?** Simple, direct, effective.

### How AI Helps

**Ask AI:**
> "Write a cold email using the PSA framework for [lead details]. Problem: [their likely challenge]. Solution: [how we help]. Action: [what we want them to do]. Keep it under 150 words."

**What you get:**
- Structured, effective email
- Clear value proposition
- Natural language
- Ready to send

## Your Practice Exercise: The Cold Email Rewrite

Ready to transform a bad email into a good one? Let's do it!

### The Challenge

Take a terrible cold email you've received (or write a generic one). Use AI to rewrite it using the PSA framework.

### Step 1: Find or Write a Bad Email (5 minutes)

**Bad email example:**

**Why it's bad:**

**Review the rewrite:**
- Is it personalised?

- Does it sound natural?
- Would you reply to it?

### Step 3: Compare and Learn (10 minutes)

**Compare:**
- Original vs. rewritten
- What changed?
- Why is the new version better?
- What can you learn?

### Questions to Reflect On

- Can you automate this for 100 leads?
- What makes the new version effective?

## Multi-Channel Sequences (Email, LinkedIn, Phone)

Don't just rely on email. Use multiple channels for better results.

### The Multi-Channel Approach

**Channel 1: Email**
- Initial outreach
- Follow-ups
- Value-add content

**Channel 2: LinkedIn**
- Connection request
- Personal message
- Content engagement

**Channel 3: Phone**
- After email/LinkedIn engagement
- For high-value leads
- When they show interest

### How to Coordinate with AI

**Ask AI:**
> "Create a 5-touch multi-channel sequence for [lead type]. Include:
> - Email 1 (initial outreach)
> - LinkedIn connection request message
> - Email 2 (follow-up with value)
> - LinkedIn message (after connection)
> - Phone call script (if they engage)
> 
> Make each touch personal and valuable. Don't be pushy."

**What you get:**
- Coordinated sequence
- Personalised for each channel
- Natural progression
- Ready to use

*'Multi-Channel: Email + LinkedIn + Phone. Result: Higher response rates. AI: I can help you coordinate all channels. You: Perfect system!'*

## Common Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

### Mistake #1: Generic Personalisation

**The problem:** Using `{{FirstName}}` and thinking it's personalised

**The solution:** Use AI to create truly personalised content based on real data about the lead.

### Mistake #2: Too Many Touches Too Fast

**The problem:** Bombarding leads with messages

**The solution:** Space out touches. Give them time to respond. Use AI to plan timing.

### Mistake #3: Not Following Up

**The problem:** Sending one email and giving up

**The solution:** Use sequences. Most replies come after 3-5 touches. AI can help you plan follow-ups.

### Mistake #4: Ignoring Intent Signals

**The problem:** Treating all leads the same

**The solution:** Score leads. Focus on hot ones. Use AI to identify intent signals.

*'Mistake: Generic emails to everyone. Result: Low response rates. Solution: Personalised sequences with AI. Result: Higher engagement!'*
