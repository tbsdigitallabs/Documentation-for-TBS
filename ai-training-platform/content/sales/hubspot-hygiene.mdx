## Technique 1: The Business Card Scanner (Stop Typing Names)

Stop manually typing information from business cards. Let AI do it!

### The Old Way (Why It's Painful)

**Traditional approach:**
1. Get a business card
2. Open HubSpot
3. Type name, title, company, email, phone
4. Repeat for every card
5. Takes forever
6. Easy to make mistakes

**Time:** 5 minutes per card Ã— 20 cards = 100 minutes wasted!

### The New Way (Automated)

**Step 1:** Scan the card

**Tools:**
- HubSpot Mobile App (has built-in scanner)
- Dedicated AI scanner apps
- Camera + AI extraction

**Step 2:** AI extracts information

**What AI does:**
- Reads the card
- Extracts: Name, Title, Company, Email, Phone
- Formats it correctly
- Ready to import

**Step 3:** Automatic enrichment

**Workflow:**
1. AI extracts basic info
2. Zapier/Make triggers enrichment
3. Looks up: Company Size, Industry, Location (using Clearbit/Apollo)
4. Adds all data to HubSpot automatically

**Time:** 30 seconds per card Ã— 20 cards = 10 minutes total!

**Time saved:** 90 minutes!

*'Old Way: Type everything manually. New Way: Scan, AI extracts, auto-enriches. Time: 30 seconds vs 5 minutes. You: This is the future!'*

## Technique 2: Email Sentiment Analysis (Who to Call First)

You have 50 open deals. Who do you call? AI can help you prioritise.

### The Problem

**The situation:**
- 50 deals in your pipeline
- Not enough time to call everyone
- Need to prioritise
- Hard to know who's hot and who's not

**The solution:**
Use AI to analyse email sentiment and identify buying intent.

### How It Works

**Step 1:** Export recent emails from your deals

**Step 2:** Ask AI:
> "Read the last email from these 10 leads. Score their 'Buying Intent' from 1-10. Flag any objections (e.g., 'Budget', 'Timing'). Suggest the top 3 to call today."

**What you get:**
- Buying intent scores
- Identified objections
- Prioritised call list
- Focus on hot leads

**Example output:**
> "**Top 3 to Call Today:**
> 1. Sarah at Acme Corp (Intent: 9/10) - Said 'ready to move forward'
> 2. Mike at TechCo (Intent: 8/10) - Asked about implementation timeline
> 3. Lisa at StartupX (Intent: 7/10) - Mentioned budget approval
> 
> **Objections Found:**
> - Budget: 3 leads
> - Timing: 2 leads
> - Need more info: 5 leads"

**See?** Now you know exactly who to call!

*'50 Deals: *all seem important* AI Analysis: *prioritises* You: Now I know who to call first. Result: Better use of time, higher close rates!'*

## Technique 3: The "Stalled Deal" Wake-Up (Automating Follow-Ups)

Deals rot in the "Proposal Sent" stage. Automate wake-up calls!

### The Problem

**Common scenario:**
- Deal sits in "Proposal Sent" for 14+ days
- No activity
- You forget to follow up
- Deal goes cold
- Opportunity lost

**The solution:**
Automate wake-up emails for stalled deals.

### How to Set It Up

**Automation rule:**
- **Trigger:** Deal in "Proposal Sent" stage for > 14 days with no activity
- **Action:** AI drafts a "check-in" email

**The AI prompt:**
> "Draft a check-in email for [Deal Name]. Reference the specific proposal we sent on [Date]. Mention [specific details from proposal]. Keep it friendly, not pushy. Ask if they have questions or need anything."

**What you get:**
- Personalised follow-up email
- References specific proposal
- Natural, not robotic
- Task created for you to review and send

**Result:**
- Stalled deals get attention
- No deals fall through cracks
- Automated, but still personal
- Saves time

*'Stalled Deal: *sits for 14 days* Automation: *drafts wake-up email* You: *reviews and sends* Deal: *comes back to life* You: Automation that actually works!'*

## Your Practice Exercise: The Pipeline Purge

Ready to clean up your pipeline? Let's do it!

### The Challenge

Clean up your HubSpot pipeline using AI to identify issues and opportunities.

### Step 1: Audit Closed Lost Reasons (15 minutes)

**What to do:**
1. Export your "Closed Lost" deals from the last quarter
2. List the reasons they gave

**Ask AI:**
> "Analyse these closed-lost reasons: [list reasons]. What is the most common reason we lose deals? Is there a pattern in the company size or industry? What should we focus on to improve?"

**What you get:**
- Understanding of why you lose deals
- Patterns you might have missed
- Actionable insights
- Areas to improve

### Step 2: Analyse Patterns (10 minutes)

**Ask AI:**
> "Based on the closed-lost data, is there a pattern? Do we lose more deals from certain industries? Company sizes? What does this tell us about our ideal customer?"

**What you get:**
- Pattern recognition
- Better understanding of your ICP
- Data to refine your targeting
- Strategic insights

### Step 3: Clean Duplicates (10 minutes)

**What to do:**
1. Export your contacts
2. Ask AI to identify duplicates

**Or use HubSpot's built-in duplicate finder**, but AI can help you decide which records to keep:

> "These contacts appear to be duplicates: [list]. Which record has the most complete information? Which should we keep as the primary?"

**What you get:**
- Clean contact list
- No duplicate confusion
- Better data quality
- More accurate reporting

### Step 4: Set Up Automation (5 minutes)

**What to do:**
Draft the logic for a "Stalled Deal" automation:
- When: Deal in "Proposal Sent" > 14 days, no activity
- Action: AI drafts check-in email
- Result: Task for you to review and send

**Even if you don't set it up now**, you have the plan!

### Questions to Reflect On

- Did the analysis reveal a weakness in your pitch?
- How many duplicates did you find?
- Did the "Wake-Up" email draft sound human?
- How much time would automation save you?

*'Messy Pipeline: *exists* AI Analysis: *finds issues* Clean Pipeline: *organised* You: Finally, data I can actually use!'*

## Automating Data Enrichment (Complete Records Automatically)

Don't manually research every contact. Let AI enrich them automatically.

### How It Works

**Step 1:** New contact added to HubSpot

**Step 2:** Automation triggers:
- Look up company information
- Add: Industry, Company Size, Location, Tech Stack
- Enrich contact: Social profiles, recent news

**Step 3:** AI summarises the enrichment:
> "Summarise this company: [enrichment data]. What are their likely pain points? What's their growth trajectory? What should our sales team know?"

**What you get:**
- Complete contact records
- Company insights
- Sales intelligence
- Ready for personalised outreach

**All automatic!** No manual research needed.

*'New Contact: *basic info only* Auto-Enrichment: *adds company data* Complete Record: *ready for sales* You: No manual research needed!'*

## Common Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

### Mistake #1: Not Enriching Contacts

**The problem:** Incomplete contact records

**The solution:** Set up automatic enrichment. Let AI add company data, industry, size, etc.

### Mistake #2: Ignoring Stalled Deals

**The problem:** Deals sit in pipeline forever

**The solution:** Automate wake-up emails. Use AI to draft personalised follow-ups.

### Mistake #3: Not Analysing Lost Deals

**The problem:** Repeating the same mistakes

**The solution:** Use AI to analyse closed-lost reasons. Find patterns. Improve your approach.

### Mistake #4: Manual Data Entry

**The problem:** Wasting time typing information

**The solution:** Automate everything possible. Use scanners, enrichment, AI extraction.

*'Mistake: Manual data entry, ignoring stalled deals. Result: Wasted time, lost opportunities. Solution: Automate with AI. Result: Clean data, active pipeline!'*
