## Technique 1: Email Triage (From 50 Emails to 5 Minutes)

Let's start with the biggest time-sink: email.

### The Problem

**The situation:**
- 50 unread emails in your inbox
- Some need replies
- Some are newsletters
- Some are urgent
- Some can be ignored
- Sorting through them takes forever

**The old way:**
- Read each email
- Decide what to do
- Write replies
- File or delete
- Takes 1-2 hours

### The AI Solution

**Step 1:** Paste 5-10 emails into AI

**Step 2:** Ask AI:
> "I'm going to paste 5 emails. For each one:
> - Draft a short, polite reply (if needed)
> - If it's a newsletter, just say 'Summary: [One Sentence]'
> - If it requires action, flag it as 'URGENT'
> - If it can be ignored, say 'Archive'"

**What you get:**
- Drafted replies ready to send
- Newsletter summaries
- Urgent items flagged
- Clear action items
- All in minutes!

**Example output:**
> "**Email 1:** Reply drafted: 'Thanks for the update! I'll review and get back to you by Friday.'
> 
> **Email 2:** Newsletter - Summary: 'New AI tools for productivity released this month.'
> 
> **Email 3:** URGENT - Action required: Meeting request needs response
> 
> **Email 4:** Archive - No action needed
> 
> **Email 5:** Reply drafted: 'Happy to help! Let's schedule a call.'"

**See?** 50 emails processed in 5 minutes instead of 2 hours!

*'50 Unread Emails: *overwhelming* AI Triage: *processes in 5 minutes* You: *replies ready* Result: Inbox cleared, time saved!'*

## Technique 3: The "TL;DR" Button (Summarise Everything)

Long documents don't have to take forever to read.

### The Problem

**Common scenario:**
- 50-page PDF attachment
- Long email threads
- Research papers
- Reports and documents
- Reading them takes hours

**The solution:**
Use AI to summarise and extract key information.

### The AI Solution

**How It Works**

**Step 1:** Upload document to AI (Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT)

**Step 2:** Ask AI:
> "Summarise this document. What are the 3 things I actually need to know? Are there any deadlines mentioned? What action items are there?"

**What you get:**
- Quick summary
- Key points extracted
- Deadlines identified
- Action items listed
- All in seconds!

**Example:**
> "**Summary:**
> This document outlines Q4 marketing strategy. Key points:
> 1. Focus on digital channels (social media, email)
> 2. Budget increased by 20% for Q4
> 3. New campaign launches October 15
> 
> **Deadlines:**
> - Campaign brief due: September 30
> - Launch date: October 15
> 
> **Action Items:**
> - Review campaign brief
> - Approve budget increase
> - Schedule launch meeting"

**See?** 50-page document summarised in 30 seconds!

*'50-Page Document: *overwhelming* AI Summary: *3 key points* You: *understands in 30 seconds* Result: Time saved, clarity gained!'*

## Your Practice Exercise: The Time Audit

Ready to reclaim your time? Let's do a time audit!

### The Challenge

Identify where you're wasting time on admin, then automate one task.

### Step 1: Audit Your Calendar (10 minutes)

**What to do:**
1. Look at your calendar for last week
2. Identify "Admin Bloat" (tasks that required low brain power)
3. List them

**Examples:**
- Email sorting and replies
- Meeting scheduling
- Document filing
- Data entry
- Report formatting

### Step 2: Identify the Biggest Time-Sink (5 minutes)

**Ask yourself:**
- Which task took the most time?
- Which task is most repetitive?
- Which task could be automated?

**Pick ONE** to automate first.

### Step 3: Build an AI Workflow (15 minutes)

**For your chosen task, ask AI:**
> "I spend [X] hours per week on [task]. How can I automate this using AI? Give me a step-by-step workflow."

**Example:**
> "I spend 2 hours per week writing weekly update emails. How can I automate this?"

**AI response:**
> "**Workflow:**
> 1. Export your calendar events for the week
> 2. Export your completed tasks
> 3. Paste into AI with prompt: 'Create a weekly update email from this data. Include: Accomplishments, challenges, next week's priorities.'
> 4. Review and send
> 
> **Time saved:** 2 hours → 10 minutes"

**Build your workflow** and test it!

### Questions to Reflect On

- How much time did you spend on email last week?
- Did the AI summary miss anything important?
- How does it feel to have a "Deep Work" block protected?
- What other tasks could you automate?

*'Time Audit: *identifies waste* AI Workflow: *automates* Result: Hours saved per week. You: This is life-changing!'*
