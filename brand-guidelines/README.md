# TBS Digital Labs Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

TBS Digital Labs is an Australian digital agency specialising in innovative technology solutions and AI integration. We help businesses transform their operations through cutting-edge digital strategies and AI-powered workflows.

## Brand Values

- **Innovation**: Pioneering new technologies and methodologies
- **Excellence**: Delivering exceptional quality in all our work
- **Collaboration**: Working closely with clients as partners
- **Integrity**: Transparent, honest, and ethical practices
- **Growth**: Continuous learning and improvement

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Australian businesses seeking digital transformation
- **Secondary**: International companies looking for innovative solutions
- **Internal**: TBS Digital Labs team members across all roles

## Brand Personality

- Professional yet approachable
- Technologically advanced
- Reliable and trustworthy
- Forward-thinking
- Australian identity with global reach

## Visual Identity

### Primary Logo
- TBS Digital Labs primary logo with accent stars
- **"TBS"** in smaller text positioned above
- **"DIGITAL LABS"** in larger, bold sans-serif font
- **Accent stars**: Four-pointed star symbols replacing dots in "DIGITAL" and "LABS"
- **Star colours**: Light Magenta (`#D56EED`) for "DIGITAL", Sage (`#61BDBA`) for "LABS"

### Logo Variations

#### Primary Logo Versions
1. **Dark Background (Oxford Blue)**
   - Background: Oxford Blue (`#02022B`)
   - Logo text: Off-White (`#FCFCFC`)
   - Accent stars: Light Magenta (`#D56EED`) and Sage (`#61BDBA`)

2. **Dark Background (Black)**
   - Background: Black (`#000000`)
   - Logo text: White (`#FFFFFF`)
   - Accent stars: Light Magenta (`#D56EED`) and Sage (`#61BDBA`)

#### Secondary Logo Versions
1. **Light Background (Off-White)**
   - Background: Off-White (`#FCFCFC`)
   - Logo text: Oxford Blue (`#02022B`)
   - Accent stars: Light Magenta (`#D56EED`) and Sage (`#61BDBA`)

2. **Light Background (White)**
   - Background: White (`#FFFFFF`)
   - Logo text: Black (`#000000`)
   - Accent stars: Light Magenta (`#D56EED`) and Sage (`#61BDBA`)

### Alternative Logo
- Simplified version for smaller applications
- Maintains brand recognition
- Flexible usage across platforms

### Typography
- **Headings**: Funnel Display (Variable weight font: 300-800)
- **Body Text**: Rethink Sans (Variable weight font: 400-800)
- Both fonts support multiple weights for hierarchy

### Official Colour Palette

#### Primary Colours
- **Oxford Blue** `#02022B` - Primary brand colour (headers, CTAs, main UI elements)
- **Ultramarine** `#121856` - Secondary brand colour (accents, depth, variations)

#### Secondary Colours
- **Off-White** `#FCFCFC` - Secondary colour (backgrounds, light text on dark)
- **Dark Grey** `#111111` - Alternative/text colour (body text, dark backgrounds)

#### Accent Colours
- **Light Magenta** `#D56EED` - Accent colour (creative roles, highlights, interactive elements)
- **Sage** `#61BDBA` - Accent colour (completion states, success indicators, balance)

## Usage Guidelines

- Always maintain brand consistency across all touchpoints
- Use Australian English spelling and terminology
- Respect minimum sizes and clear space requirements (20px minimum clear space around logo)
- Ensure accessibility compliance (4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum)
- Adapt for different contexts while maintaining brand integrity
- Use SVG logos for digital, PNG for web, AI/EPS for print
- Never distort, rotate, or modify logo proportions
