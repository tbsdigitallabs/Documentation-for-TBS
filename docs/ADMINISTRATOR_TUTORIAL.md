## What Is LearningLab?

**In plain English:** LearningLab is an online training platform where TBS Digital Labs team members learn how to use AI tools in their jobs.

Think of it like:
- Netflix for training videos ‚Üí but with slides and quizzes
- LinkedIn Learning ‚Üí but specifically for our team and AI tools
- A video game ‚Üí where people earn points and level up as they complete training

### Who Uses It?

| Role | What They Learn |
|------|-----------------|
| Developers | AI coding tools, automation, testing |
| Designers | AI design tools, asset generation |
| Project Managers | AI for planning, meetings, workflows |
| Content Creators | AI for writing, video, PR |
| Sales & Business Dev | AI for leads, CRM, reporting |

### How It Works (The Big Picture)

1. **Team member logs in** ‚Üí using their work Google account
2. **They pick their role** ‚Üí Developer, Designer, PM, etc.
3. **They complete foundation training** ‚Üí Everyone does this first
4. **They access role-specific modules** ‚Üí Training tailored to their job
5. **They earn XP and level up** ‚Üí Gamification keeps it fun
6. **They appear on the leaderboard** ‚Üí Friendly competition

## Getting Started

### How to Log In

1. **Go to** the LearningLab website (your IT team will give you the link)
2. **Click** the blue **"Continue with Google"** button
3. **Choose** your work Google account (must be a TBS domain email)
4. **Done!** You're now logged in

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Note:** Only people with TBS email addresses can log in. If someone needs access with a different email, see [Adding Exception Emails](#adding-exception-emails).

### First-Time Setup (Onboarding)

When someone logs in for the first time, they'll see a series of questions:

| Step | What They See | What to Do |
|------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | "What's your role?" | Click their job type (Developer, Designer, etc.) |
| 2 | "Experience level?" | Click Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced |
| 3 | "Upload a picture" | Upload a photo or click "Generate Avatar" |
| 4 | Done! | They're taken to the training hub |

## Managing Team Members

### Who Can Access the Platform?

By default, only people with these email domains can log in:
- @thebigsmoke.com
- @thebigsmoke.com.au
- @tbsdigitallabs.com
- @tbsdigitallabs.com.au

### Adding Exception Emails

If someone needs access but doesn't have a TBS email:

1. **Go to** the Admin page (`/admin`)
2. **Type** their email address in the text box
3. **Click** the **"Add"** button
4. **Repeat** for any other emails
5. **Click** the **"Save Changes"** button

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** Changes may take a few minutes to take effect.

### Removing Exception Emails

1. **Go to** the Admin page
2. **Find** the email you want to remove
3. **Click** the red **trash icon** next to it
4. **Click** **"Save Changes"**

## Understanding the Training System

### How XP (Experience Points) Works

| Action | XP Earned |
|--------|-----------|
| Complete a module presentation | Varies by module |
| Finish a quiz | Based on quiz length |
| Get a question right | Bonus XP |

**You earn XP even if you get quiz questions wrong** ‚Äì the goal is learning, not perfection.

### Level System

| Level | XP Required | Rank Name |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | 0 | Initiate |
| 2 | 100 | Rookie |
| 3 | 250 | Operative |
| 4 | 500 | Specialist |
| 5 | 1,000 | Veteran |
| 6 | 2,000 | Elite |
| 7 | 3,500 | Expert |
| 8 | 5,000 | Commander |
| 9 | 7,500 | Legend |
| 10 | 10,000 | Apex |

### Rewards and Cosmetics

As team members level up, they unlock visual rewards:

| Reward Type | What It Is |
|-------------|------------|
| Frames | Borders around profile picture |
| Badges | Icons displayed next to name |
| Effects | Animations around avatar |
| Nameplates | Visual styles for username |
| Titlebars | Banners above username |
| Titles | Special words below name |

Team members can choose which rewards to display on their profile.

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### "I can't log in"

| Possible Cause | Solution |
|----------------|----------|
| Wrong email domain | Ask admin to add your email as an exception |
| Google account not signed in | Sign into Google first, then try again |
| Browser issue | Clear cache and cookies, try again |
| Using wrong browser | Try Chrome or Firefox |

### "My progress isn't saving"

| Possible Cause | Solution |
|----------------|----------|
| Lost internet connection | Check your connection and refresh |
| Browser blocking storage | Enable cookies for the site |
| Session expired | Log out and log back in |

### "I completed a module but it doesn't show"

| Try This | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Refresh the page | Sometimes updates take a moment |
| Log out and back in | Refreshes your session |
| Wait 5 minutes | Server sync can take time |

### "The leaderboard shows wrong information"

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Data sync delay | Wait a few minutes and refresh |
| Browser cache | Clear browser cache or use incognito mode |

### "Quiz won't submit my answer"

| Try This |
|----------|
| Click directly on the answer text |
| Wait for the button to become active |
| Try refreshing the page (you may need to restart the quiz) |

### "I can't access the Admin page"

| Possible Cause | Solution |
|----------------|----------|
| Your email isn't in admin list | Contact another admin or IT |
| Not logged in | Log in first, then go to /admin |

### "Someone's avatar won't load"

| Try This |
|----------|
| They can upload a new image |
| They can generate a new avatar |
| Clear your browser cache |

## Tips and Best Practices

### Do's ‚úÖ

| Do... | Because... |
|-------|------------|
| Complete Session 0 first | It's required before role training |
| Encourage team members to update their profiles | Makes the leaderboard more engaging |
| Check the leaderboard regularly | Spot who might need encouragement |
| Let people learn at their own pace | Better retention than rushing |
| Celebrate milestones | Reaching new levels is worth acknowledging |

### Don'ts ‚ùå

| Don't... | Because... |
|----------|------------|
| Share login credentials | Each person needs their own account for tracking |
| Skip the quizzes | They reinforce learning, even if scores are low |
| Add exception emails for non-team members | Security risk |
| Worry about perfect quiz scores | Learning matters more than perfection |
| Expect instant updates | Some changes take a few minutes to sync |
